WELCOME TO LE COMPTOIR MEZZE

To sum up who we are in one sentence :

It’s a convenient lunch break, easily personalisable, healthy and original in the heart of Paris famous business district for fancy and busy workers.
Why to choose us ?
Simply because we are :
- Original
- Quality
- Tailored, ok for everyone -> vegies, meat guys,... Open to every consumer
- Mobile truck
- Faster to deliver and healthy

Our top 3 of the main performance objectives:
1. Time (service time, wait time)
2. Flexibility (menu customization, adaptation to demand)
3. Quality (freshness, taste, presentation)

Now some Q&A about us

• Where in La Défense will you park?
Between the Grande Arche and the Bassin de Takis (mettre une carte)

• How will you design the truck layout?
Queue on the esplanade / In line flux like Subway with differents meals available OR Clik and collect. We can prepare in advance if you ordered before.
Our equipement : Truc, cold boxes, heaters

• How will customers order and receive food?
In line flux like Subway with differents meals available OR Clik and collect. We can prepare in advance if you ordered before.

• How will you source ingredients? (suppliers, frequency, quality standards)
We will only buy our ingredients from local producers, short approvisonement and made in France. Also we will be fully transparent on the origins of our products.

• What inventory strategy will you use? (freshness, waste management)
Just in time strategy, low stocks, daily preparations on the morning.

• How will you manage daily preparation?
Order stock everyday so it remains fresh.

• Can you map the typical customer journey: from arrival to receiving food
So you can scan our QR code when arriving or you can use our app or our internet site, then you will order online and finally pick up at the truck.

• How will you optimize flow and reduce waiting time?
Thanks to our QR code and our app

• What steps can be standardized vs. customized?
The way to order by QR code or directly thanks to the QR code is standardized, but the meal is 100% personalisable.
Every portion is the same, serving spoons are stadardized to put the same amount of product in the meals.

• How many employees do you have per shift?
We have 2 or 3 employees per shift.

• What skills are required? (cooking, service, speed, customer interaction)
At the same time we will have 1 person serving, 1 cooking and heating and 1 billing. Our main objective is the integration of disabled employees.

• How will you train them?
3 days formation, everyone is trained for every post to be fully polyvalent.

• What POS system will you use?
We will use an Alipay like system

• How will you handle payments? (cash, card, mobile)
You can pay thanks to 3 different ways by cash, by Card and thanks to your mobile

• Will you have an app for pre-ordering?
Yes, we will have our own app like Alipay and Ubereat

• How do your operations decisions support your value proposition?
In our opinion it is important to hire the right people and to be inclusive.
Also we want to boost our employees to be efficient and happy in their work.

• How do they support your performance priorities?
POS support the fast delivery.
Buying at local producers support quality.

• What are the main risks or challenges you foresee?
Risk of a too long waiting queue
Risk of products out of stocks
The weather
